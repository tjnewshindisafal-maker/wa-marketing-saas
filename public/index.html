FAQ
Common Questions
Everything you need to know
Will my WhatsApp number get banned?
We send messages with 2-3 second delays between each message, mimicking human behavior. This keeps your number safe. We've had 500+ businesses use our tool without any ban issues.
Do I need WhatsApp Business API?
No! That's the best part. We connect directly to your regular WhatsApp via QR code scan. No Meta verification, no API setup, no extra cost. Just scan and start sending.
Can customers reply to my messages?
Yes! Messages go from YOUR WhatsApp number. When customers reply, the reply comes to YOUR WhatsApp directly. You can chat with them normally.
What is the Shop Dashboard / Service Plan?
It's a complete job management system for salons, repair shops, service centers, clinics, gyms, and similar businesses. Create jobs/appointments, send auto WhatsApp updates to customers, generate invoices — all in one place.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All paid plans come with a 7-day free trial. You get full access to all features. No credit card required. Just sign up and start using.
