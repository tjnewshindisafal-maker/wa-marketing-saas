Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WA Marketing Pro
What is WA Marketing Pro?
WA Marketing Pro is India's affordable bulk WhatsApp marketing tool. You can send messages with images directly from your own WhatsApp number to 500+ customers using Excel upload. No WhatsApp Business API needed.
How much does WA Marketing Pro cost?
Plans start at just ₹499/month for Starter (50 messages/day), ₹999 for Pro (200 messages/day), ₹1,499 for Service Hub (job tracking + invoicing), and ₹2,499 for Business (500 messages/day).
Do I need WhatsApp Business API?
No. Just scan a QR code once with your WhatsApp. Messages go from YOUR number so your business name shows up with the customer, not ours.
Which businesses can use WA Marketing Pro?
Perfect for repair shops, salons, clinics, auto garages, electricians, plumbers, real estate agents, restaurants, courier services, and e-commerce stores across India.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Get a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. Start sending bulk WhatsApp messages immediately after sign-up.
Can I send images with bulk WhatsApp messages?
Yes! You can send product photos, festival greetings, offer banners directly in WhatsApp. No links — real images are delivered to every customer.
Is bulk WhatsApp messaging safe for my number?
We use smart delays and rate limits to minimize ban risk. However, for high-volume messaging (500+ daily), we recommend using a dedicated business number, not your personal WhatsApp.
