Common questions

Honest answers to what everyone asks.

Still have a question? WhatsApp us at +91 80872 89267.

Kya mera WhatsApp number ban ho sakta hai?
+
Honest answer: Agar aap din ke 1000+ messages bhejenge bina delay ke, haan risk hai — kisi bhi tool se. Humne built-in safety rakhi hai: message delays, daily limits per plan (50–500), aur natural sending patterns. 6 mahine se chal rahi aapki business WhatsApp safely use ho rahi hai 500+ shops mein. Starter plan pe 50 messages/day ke saath chance nearly zero hai.
Kitne customers ko message bhej sakta hoon?
+
Plan ke hisaab se: Starter 50/day, Pro 200/day, Service 200/day, Business 500/day. Monthly totals: 1,500 / 6,000 / 6,000 / 15,000 messages. Zaroorat zyada ho toh Business plan ya custom quote.
Mera data safe hai? Customer list kahan save hoti hai?
+
Haan. Aapka data encrypted MongoDB mein stored hai, India-based servers pe. Hum aapke contacts kisi ko share nahi karte, na koi ads ke liye use karte hain. Aap kabhi bhi account delete kar sakte ho — saara data 24 hours mein remove.
Free trial mein credit card chahiye?
+
Nahi. Sirf name, email, phone number. 7 din bina kisi payment ke full access. Trial ke baad agar achcha laga toh UPI se pay karo, nahi toh account automatically Starter limits pe chala jayega. No auto-charge. No surprise.
Setup mein kitna time lagta hai?
+
Signup se first message bhejne tak — 2 minutes. QR code scan karo apne WhatsApp se (jaise WhatsApp Web), contact list upload karo, message likho, send. Humari onboarding call bhi free hai agar chahiye toh — 15 min mein setup kara denge.
Refund policy kya hai?
+
7-day free trial hai hi — tabhi test kar lo properly. Uske baad agar kisi reason se dissatisfied ho, first 7 days mein full refund (monthly plan). Setup fee non-refundable hai. Annual plans pe pro-rated refund.
WhatsApp Business app se different kaise hai?
+
WhatsApp Business app mein aapko ek-ek karke sabko message karna padta hai, ya phir "broadcast list" banani padti hai (jo sirf unhi contacts tak jaati hai jo aapka number save kiya ho). Hum Excel upload pe 500 logon ko personalized message bhej dete hain — unka naam, product image, sab kuch — bina har customer ka number save kiye.
